What is Hatha Yoga ?

Hatha yoga is a branch of yoga primarily concerned with mastery of the body, and it is arguably the tradition most familiar to Western culture.
The term is derived from the Sanskrit ha, meaning "sun," and tha, meaning "moon," leading to the common interpretation that Hatha practices are designed to unite and balance these two energies.
However, the literal translation of Hatha is "force," alluding to the physical techniques used within this tradition.
