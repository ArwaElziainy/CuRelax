Who Are We

CuRelax

CuRelax is a website application that helps people who need care and suffer from mild to moderate mental illness, by providing them with yoga videos, courses, positive quotes, recommend games, books and movies, in addition to successful healing stories of people who were suffering from mental illness.
Also, Curelax provides recommendations to people who suffer from severe/extreme mental illness to go to Mental Health centers whom the platform partnered with, so they give them the best offers.
